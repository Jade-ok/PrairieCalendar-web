Privacy Policy for PrairieCalendar

Last Updated: March 2026

PrairieCalendar does not collect, sell, or share any personal data with third parties.

1. Data Collection and Usage

The extension reads reservation information from the PrairieTest webpage (specifically us.prairietest.com) only when the user opens the extension. This information may include exam titles, dates, times, and locations. All processed data is stored locally using Chrome’s storage.local API.

If you choose to download your schedule as an .ics (iCalendar) file, the file is generated entirely locally on your device without communicating with any external servers.

PrairieCalendar does not:

  • Track browsing activity
  • Collect analytics
  • Use cookies
  • Share data with third parties

2. Google Calendar Export

If the user chooses to export schedules to Google Calendar, the extension requests permission to access Google Calendar via OAuth 2.0. Authentication is handled entirely by Google. The extension only writes exam schedule data (title, date, time, location) to the user’s own Google Calendar. No data is stored on external servers or shared with third parties.

3. Google API Services User Data Policy (Limited Use)

PrairieCalendar's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

4. Data Retention

All locally stored data remains on the user’s device and can be cleared at any time by removing the extension or clearing Chrome extension storage.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this policy, please contact: team-blueberrypie@googlegroups.com
